My Story

Graduating from AUT in 2004 with a Bachelor of Health Science (Physiotherapy) I have over 15 years experience as a Physiotherapist. In this time I have worked within Community Neurological Paediatrics, Orthopaedics, Adult Neurology, Traumatic Brain Injury/Concussion Community Rehab, and Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy.

In 2015 I attended a Myofascial Release Therapy course, and have used this technique in my practice ever since. I completed advanced MFR courses and became a Certified Myofascial Release Practitioner in 2018 (Certified under Beth Beauchamp - Medical Massage Therapist).

I have had my own physiotherapy private practice in Waiuku since 2015, however I have now moved away from providing 'physiotherapy' and as of 2020, focus solely on Myofascial Release Therapy.
