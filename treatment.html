Treatment

Myofascial Release therapy is a specialised and specific ‘hands on’ technique or ‘manual therapy’. MFR is similar to ‘massage’, but the technique is performed without the use of any lotions or oils. This allows the therapists hands to ‘sink’ into the clients body in order to connect with the fascia ‘through’ the skin. A gentle pressure is applied in order to slowly stretch and release the fascia. The treatments start superficially – working the fascia just under the skin – as the fascia releases the treatment can begin to have an effect through into the deeper fascial layers around and within the muscles.

MFR is an extremely effective and safe technique, and can be used to treat both acute and chronic injuries or dysfunctions. Some examples of things MFR can treat are:

  • Back Pain
  • Neck Pain e.g. Whiplash
  • Headache
  • Migraine
  • Jaw Pain/TMJ Syndrome
  • Sciatica
  • Joint Pain e.g. pain from Osteoarthritis
  • Nerve Pain e.g. Thoracic Outlet Syndrome
  • Stress Related Pain e.g. Chronic Fatigue Syndrome, Fibromyalgia
  • Musculoskeletal Injuries – acute and chronic
  • And much, much more!!

The first session will be 60 minutes to allow time for Initial consultation and some treatment. From there we can discuss the plan for treatment going forward.

Please feel free to contact me if you have any questions or queries regarding your specific issue and how MFR could help you.
